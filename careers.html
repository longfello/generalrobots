Electrical Machine Builder

Responsible for building electrical panels, field wiring and pneumatic plumbing components to print. Follows processes as specified in company policies/instructions. Meets schedule and quality standards. Maintains tooling and equipment expertise. Continuous self-improvement. Troubleshoots and resolves problems.

Essential Duties And Responsibilities
  • Reads and interprets complex blueprints, electrical and pneumatic schematic, or engineering instructions to build electronic components, panels, subassemblies, and wiring.
  • Completes electronic builds using appropriate hardware, soldering and hand and/or power tools.
  • Assists in systems integration, PLC, robot, and HMI configurations.
  • Assists in troubleshooting automation manufacturing control systems consisting of PLC's, industrial networks, robots, machine vision and HMI interfaces.
  • Performs I/O checks, sets, and documents sensors, performs sensor and message challenge testing.
  • Responsible for change control documentation including marking electrical and pneumatic schematic packages with as built updates and changes.
  • Stays current in new developments within industries served. Keeps informed of new applications/methods to improve systems/products.
  • Observes all safety rules, applicable regulations, and relevant ISO standards.

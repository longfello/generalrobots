General Robots

Prototype 1.0

The prototype is set to detect wrinkled paper. It reacts to the appearance of a crumpled lump thrown into a random place in the field of view. After garbage collection, the prototype returns to its original position.

Recognizes the type of garbage.

Defines the boundaries and location of an object.

Builds a route to the object.

Picks up garbage in a container.

Returns to the place of deployment.

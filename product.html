General Robots

The neural network is trained to recognize different types of garbage: plastic bottles, aluminum cans, paper, and others. The accuracy of recognition is over 90%.

The robot has on board a modern Computer controlled by the Unix-type operating system. The Computer provides the work of Artificial Intelligence, as well as gives orders to the Microcontroller to implement a given motion program.

The Microcontroller operates the motor functions of the robot, receiving orders from the Computer in the form of preset microprograms: to go there, take trash, turn around, and so on.

Product

Robot that collects garbage

The camcorder receives a dynamic image, monitors the situation around, photographs the image of the garbage for its subsequent recognition.

The robot collects garbage along a given route, specifying its location using Wi-Fi tracking networks. This enables the robot to determine its position to within half an inch.

The rechargeable battery gives the robot the ability to work all day on a single charge without polluting the environment.
